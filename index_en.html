Premyogi Society

Welcome to the official website of the Premyogi Society association!

Mission

Premyogi Society is a non-profit association that does not pursue profit, but works exclusively for the common good. It coordinates its activities at home and abroad.

The Premyogi Society association primarily pursues the goal of providing all individuals regardless of age, gender, race, belief, worldview, culture and social background with the universal love, the healing character and the helping hand of Premyogi Nitai in the material as well as the spiritual sense. It supports the general public and its members in building trust in and developing an individual relationship with Premyogi Nitai.

With its foundation and the activities derived therefrom, it strives to do justice to the ontology of Premyogi Nitai in all of its important aspects. The Premyogi Society association endeavors to support all members, friends, as well as like-minded people on their individual path through life with Premyogi Nitai in a helpful but non-binding manner.

The philosophical foundations of the Premyogi Society include tolerance, respect and non-violence in the all-encompassing sense. The association believes that it can make a significant contribution to a tolerant, peaceful and happier world.

Strategy

Premyogi Society is organized as lean and informal as possible. Its backbone is a relatively small but global network of volunteers, donors and sympathizers. Mutual trust in all matters of the association is the be-all and end-all.

Premyogi Society maintains close contact with associations with the same or a similar purpose abroad, especially in India and the USA. Its activities are constantly based on the dynamics of the ever-changing world. In the entire spectrum of its activities, the focus is primarily laid on the efficient and effective use of the internet and IT technologies. Conventional kind of charity work, however, is carried out selectively when the need and circumstances require it.
